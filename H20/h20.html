Infrastructure Development

Earlier H2O gym used to be located in a smaller area which couldn’t contain a lot of customers working out at the same time. So we shifted them to a bigger location. But the new place where the gym was supposed to be shifted was a total mess with broken floor, roof , dirty walls, broken ac vent, no rent/water/electricity management, no furniture etc.

We hired labor and fixed all the infrastructure issues and made the place look like a proper gym. We decorated the gym will wall arts, installed new workout equipments, new furniture, CCD machine.

After weeks of hardwork, we finally managed to setup H2O gym to a new location and give it a fresh new look with all gym equipments and basic facilities and we’re proud when we see the gym filled with customers working out and transforming themselves into a better version of them.

About

H2O (Health 2 Offer) Gym is a fitness destination in Model Town 2, New Delhi which has been running for quite a few years now. In order to compete with its next gen competitors, like Cult.Fit, Anytime Fitness and provide the best possible gymming experience to its customers, they hired Power2Create.
